Are you looking for a caring, fun-filled environment for your child? An environment that stimulates their young minds? Then Little Lambs Day School is the right place for you. Children between the ages of 2 and 5 (toddlers, preschoolers) are encouraged to join.

Throughout the day they will be taught how to read and write, while they also get to socialize with their peers. We also offer an afterschool program for children between the ages of 5 and 12 for parents who are still working and need a safe place for their children to stay so they can be supervised.

Nutritious meals are served that meet the requirements for healthy growing. You can expect for your child to be fed fresh fruits and vegetables (such as grapes, pears, apples, etc.), lean protein and other healthy foods.

We make our rates affordable to make it possible for all children to have a place to be taken care of and get the education they need to be prepared to enter public/private schools. We also help afterschool kids with their homework if they're having trouble in that area. Please contact us for more information.

Kids enrolled in our program will:

  • Learn the fundamentals of reading and writing:

    • They will have the opportunity to trace letters and numbers in order to write at the best of their ability

    • They will have an utmost knowledge of grammar and pronunciation

    • Kids will engage in fun activities such as reading books for kids, singing, and watching educational television shows.

  • Learn the names of shapes and colors

  • When the weather allows it, kids will be able to go to the zoo, and other fun trips (Children are required to bring a permission slip signed by a parent/guardian)
